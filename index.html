Wenzhe Li

Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence

Tsinghua University

My name is Wenzhe Li (李文哲). I am a first year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University. My advisor is Chongjie Zhang.

My research interests lie in reinforcement learning and probabilistic methods for machine learning. I have worked on topics including offline reinforcement learning, score-based generative models, and approximate inference.

I received my B.E. from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, where I was fortunate to work with Jun Zhu, Guy van den Broeck and Stefano Ermon.
