Wenzhe Li

Princeton University

My name is Wenzhe Li (李文哲). I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Princeton University, advised by Chi Jin. I received my B.E. from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, where I was fortunate to work with Chongjie Zhang, Guy Van den Broeck, and Stefano Ermon.

My research interests lie in reinforcement learning and probabilistic methods for machine learning. I have worked on topics including offline reinforcement learning, score-based generative models, and approximate inference.
